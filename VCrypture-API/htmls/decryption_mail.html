About VCrypture
VCrypture is an online service that allows you to encrypt your secret images safely and send the result via email directly to your recipient (or just back to you). VCrypture does not save any images as the operations are directly made on-the-fly in RAM. We offer four visual cryptographic algorithms: Naor Shamir (binary mode), Taghaddos Latif (grayscale mode), Dhiman Kasana EVCT(N, N) and Dhiman Kasana EVCT(K, N) (both RGB mode).
